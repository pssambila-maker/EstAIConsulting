Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need any prior experience with AI or programming?

+

No! This course is specifically designed for complete beginners. We start from the very basics and explain everything in plain language. No programming or technical background is required.

How much time do I need to commit each week?

+

We recommend 6-8 hours per week, which includes watching lessons (2 hours), hands-on practice (3-4 hours), and project work (2 hours). The self-paced option allows you to adjust this to your schedule.

What tools or software do I need?

+

Just a computer with internet access! All the AI tools we use are cloud-based and either free or have free tiers. We'll guide you through setting up everything you need.

Will I receive a certificate?

+

Yes! Upon completing all modules and projects, you'll receive a verified Certificate of Completion that you can add to your LinkedIn profile and resume.

What if I need to miss a live session?

+

All live sessions are recorded and available within 24 hours. You can watch them at your convenience and still ask questions in our community forum.

Yes! We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied with the course within the first two weeks, we'll provide a full refund, no questions asked.
