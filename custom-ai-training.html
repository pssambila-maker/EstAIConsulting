Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to develop a custom program?

+

Typically 3-6 weeks from initial consultation to program launch. This includes discovery, curriculum design, materials development, and logistics planning. Rush timelines are available for urgent needs.

Can you work with our proprietary data and systems?

+

Yes! We sign NDAs and work within your security requirements. We can train on your actual systems, use anonymized versions of your data, or create realistic simulations based on your environment.

What if our team has mixed skill levels?

+

We design programs to accommodate varying skill levels through multiple tracks, leveled exercises, and differentiated instruction. We can also create separate cohorts for different experience levels.

Do you provide certification?

+

Yes, all participants receive a custom certificate of completion. We can also prepare teams for industry-standard certifications or create custom certification programs aligned with your internal requirements.

What's included in post-training support?

+

Support packages are customized but typically include office hours for questions, implementation assistance, access to updated materials, and follow-up sessions. Duration and intensity are based on your needs.

Can we start with a pilot program?

+

Absolutely! Many organizations start with a pilot for one department or team, then expand based on results. This allows you to test the approach and demonstrate ROI before a larger rollout.
